Music for weddings and events

One of the finest and highly experienced string quartets in London, Carnaby Strings is the perfect choice for your wedding or corporate event. We are a stylish all female ensemble who perform with prestigious performers and ensembles including the Royal Opera House orchestra, Michael Buble, Bastille and the Royal Philharmonic Orchestra. We have a huge repertoire of pop songs, as well playing classical music, Bollywood and jazz standards. There is an option to arrange any special song for your wedding if we donâ€™t have it already, tailoring the music to your special occasion.

We play regularly for weddings and events in venues such as; The Asylum Chapel, Hedsor House, Soho House, The Wallace Collection, Fitzrovia Chapel, and many of the top London hotels such as the Ritz, Grosvenor House and The Nomad Hotel just to name but a few.

We have built an excellent reputation for our skill and professionalism, being music graduates from Royal Academy of Music, Royal Northern College of Music and Trinity Laban. Other playing experience includes English National Opera Orchestra, BBC Concert Orchestra and West End shows such as Mary Poppins, Kinky Boots and most recently Hamilton and My Fair Lady.
